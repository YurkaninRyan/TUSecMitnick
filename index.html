Enter Kevin Mitnick

Social Engineering Pioneer

Early Life

  • Grew up in Los Angeles and attended James Monroe High School.
  • Was enrolled in both Los Angeles Pierce College and USC.
  • Worked as a receptionist for Stephen S. Wise Temple.
  • At the age of 15 he discovered Social Engineering and began taking advantage of various systems.
  • Let's go down the rabbit hole...

Age 14 - A bus driver told him where he could buy his own ticket punch; afterwards he would dumpster dive to find blank tickets. Now, he could ride the bus for free whenever he wanted.


Age 16 - Kevin gained unauthorized access to a computer network after finding the phone number for Ark, a computer system used by Digital Equipment Corporation. He proceeded to copy and steal all of their software.


Age 18 - Mitnick was a wanted fugitive that was on the run for breaking into Sun Microsystems, Motorola, Netcom, and Nokia. Kevin continued to social engineer his way through life until he was arrested and convicted.


Noone truly understood what Kevin Mitnick was capable of, and he became the boogeyman to the uneducated. He was accused of hacking NORAD as well as wiretapping the FBI. Rumor has it that the government requested he be put into solitary confinement because it was believed he could launch nukes by whistling into a phone.


Exit Kevin Mitnick

Kevin Mitnick is now out of jail, and owns his own computer security consulting firm. Many different books, movies, and documentaries have been made about his life.
